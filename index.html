About

missedmybestie · Est. November 2023

The Story

missedmybestie is an independent music label founded in November 2023. What started as one person managing an artist project quickly evolved into something bigger — a creative label built around French Afro culture, hoodtrap, drill, and Brazilian funk, blending these worlds into an original sound and identity.

The label was founded by two people: Mide and Marreley. Mide originally ran missedmybestie as if it were a solo artist project — producing, releasing, and managing everything himself under names like ErvoBeats and Griity Beats before converging it all under the missedmybestie umbrella. Today, he manages the label in his free time with a clear vision of building it into a fully-fledged brand.

Beyond music, the label is actively working toward original clothing and art — with plans to keep growing as new members, ideas, and sounds come together.
